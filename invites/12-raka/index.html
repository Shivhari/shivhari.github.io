Vinayak^a's Journey

Vinayak^a's journey into the world of electronic music began with a fascination for the ethereal and emotive potential of synthesized sounds.

Inspired by the pioneers of ambient and experimental electronic music, Vinayak^a has dedicated years to crafting a unique sonic palette that bridges the gap between traditional Indian melodies and contemporary electronic textures.

Vinayak^a is always looking to innovate and is a master at weaving his inner journey into a medley of sounds. Known for his raw, spontaneous and immensely powerful sets,Vinayak's Compositions are a reflection of his true self.

A Message from Vinayak^a

I want to take a moment to reflect on the profound power of music—specifically, the often-misunderstood world of electronic music.

Too often, we equate electronic music solely with the pulsating beats and loud drops we hear at nightclubs. While those moments can be exhilarating, they only scratch the surface of what this genre truly embodies.

On the 27th at Burrow Bengaluru, I plan to demonstrate that not all electronic music is hard-hitting or aggressive. Instead, I will showcase the softer, more ethereal side of this genre—music that invites you to dream, to reflect, and to feel.

Through this performance, I aim to liberate our collective sonic experience, allowing each of us to connect with the deeper layers of emotion and creativity that electronic music can offer.

So, as we gather to share this experience, I encourage you to open your hearts and minds to the infinite possibilities that await us in the sounds we will explore together.
