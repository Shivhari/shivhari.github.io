Working with me

  • You reading this note means that we might work together.
Operating principals
  • Work together = anything that is just not casual hanging out.
    Includes: ideas, feedback, projects together, startup ideas, music, even working on personal issues and general life stuff.
  • I care about my "Say/Do ratio". Say (or commit) less + Do what you say.
  • I prefer async communication. I prefer text or voice notes to in-person meetings or video calls.
    When talking about text, I prefer non-WhatsApp based text communication. WhatsApp is good for very transactional things like "how you doing" or "what's the status update".
    Anything that is more structured and requires back and forth discussion - preference is to put it on notion and share. It can be a space where we and decide and discuss.
  • I tend to prefer black and white putting down of things whether that be meeting summaries, to-dos, next steps, mind maps etc.
  • I like to take into account context that has been previously shared. Which is why I rely on written communication to quickly refresh, gather and maintain context. I also tried to be as structured and methodical about my approaches as possible. For it works remarkably well for me.
  • I sometimes am lacking in the ability to have consistency / rigor of execution. It is something that I've worked on and improved. I will always try to stick by my commitments. There might be times when I don't, and the fault of that obviously lies with me. I shall be honest about this.
    • Interpersonal stuff
  • I would appreciate transparency and honest communication always. I will always try to be accepting and non-defensive towards any conversation.
  • My preference is to not be in a place where I have to guess what is on the other person's mind.
  • I really appreciate honest feedback. I would like you to be upfront tell me if something is good or bad. We can have a discussion around why it was so. I want to be objectively as close to the truth as possible. And for that I need to hear objectivity.
    Which is also a two way street - if something is good, I would love it if you can throw in a sentence or two of how you appreciate or like something.
  • I am at a stage of my life where I cannot hold unresolved threads of relationships with people. We might choose to agree to disagree we might choose to not interact with each other but that is a better place to be than to leave things open ended and hanging because that causes anxiety and unnecessary amount of mindspace to be taken up.
    • Others
  • I, in perpetuity, sincerely apologize if any of my actions, feelings, thoughts, cause hurt or is felt as condescension or making you feel lesser. I generally mean no harm.
  • I tend to philosophize, spiritualize almost everything that I work on. I know no other framework through which I can operate as well as these. And it is not a prerequisite or necessity that you have to engage with me on either of these two levels.
  • I try to approach everything from a place of love and compassion.
    ~ Shiv.
